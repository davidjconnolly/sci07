Sci 07 plans to have its 5 year reunion the weekend of Oct 19th-21st, 2012.  We believe intermingling with current students is the most rewarding part of returning to campus.  Also we’ve arranged for ArtSci 07 to be joining us that weekend so everybody who’s anybody will be there.

We have a line-up of activities planned that represent our fondest memories of Queens.

This line-up clearly (…clearly, ah memories….) resembles a traditional Homecoming weekend, but we want to establish that we don’t view any sort of street party as part of the plan.  We want to have a fun time back at Queens but not contribute to anything that detracts from the University’s, and thus our, reputation.
